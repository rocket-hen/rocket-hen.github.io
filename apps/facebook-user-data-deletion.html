Delete your Facebook-linked data

Steelkiwi Inc. stores only:

  • your Facebook numeric ID
  • name and profile picture URL (read-only cache)
  • e-mail address (for account correspondence)

How to delete

  1. Open the mobile app → Settings › Account › Delete account.
  2. Tap Delete and confirm. We erase your profile and all Facebook-derived data within 24 h.
  3. Alternatively, e-mail privacy@rockethen.com with subject “Delete my Facebook data”.

Questions? Write to privacy@rockethen.com.
